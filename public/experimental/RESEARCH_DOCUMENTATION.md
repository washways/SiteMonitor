## Summary of what is being estimated

The experimental workflow currently estimates and summarizes:

- pumping-event duration
- pumped volume
- drawdown and maximum drawdown
- specific capacity, Q/S
- daily abstraction and pumping time
- estimated resting groundwater level
- recovery time after pumping
- rolling 7-day and 30-day changes
- borehole stress, downtime, and reliability indicators
- network-level comparison and review priority labels

## Interpretation note

These values are best treated as screening and review aids. They help identify boreholes worth further inspection, but they do not replace engineering review, field verification, or hydrogeologic context.
